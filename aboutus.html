About Us

Our story in a nutshell

The story of Svalegaarden in Hasseris

In 1952 the beautiful grocery farm was rebuilt on the corner of Thorsens Allé and Svalegårdsvej, and the farm was then renamed Svalegaarden. The idyllic farm is still at the same address, and has set the framework for many events and experiences over the years.

In June 2016, Svalegaarden got new tenants with new spirits and bright hopes for the future - Mette and Stefan from the former Restaurant Mest.

Mette and Stefan come with new energy and fresh eyes on the beautiful renovated grocery store, which has so far served as a corporate business.

Svalegaarden Mest today

Mette and Stefan have a vision of taking the best from Restaurant Mest and continuing it in the farm's beautiful setting in the heart of Aalborg.
The thoughts behind Svalegaarden Mest gastronomy meet rural idyll, where the focus is on creating memorable experiences.

The first years with Svalegaarden Mest
Svalegaarden Mest is wonderful to work with. The old buildings are felt and give us a natural humility and desire to pass on the good experiences to our guests. We have found ourselves well adapted in Hasseri's work with many different angles in gastronomy. Svalegaarden Mest includes everything from private parties, meeting facilities, lectures to food out of the house.

Food With Mest - Business lunch scheme goes solo.
Food With Mest is in a major development and more and more companies are entering the scheme. Therefore, in July 2018 we took a leap and moved this part of Svalegaarden Mest to a second and larger premises, so that Mad Med Mest can be allowed to unfold under the right conditions.
Michel Lycoops is the steward of Mad Med Mest and has been employed as a chef from Restaurant Most Origin in 2010. We are very pleased that this collaboration sees a bright future for Mad Med Mest.
